How To Catch a Fly

1 / 3
1. Locate the fly in the house (try and catch it on a window).
2 / 3
2. Grab your CLEAR plastic cup and QUICKLY cup it.
3 / 3
3. Slide a piece of paper to catch the fly under the fly and let it go out the door.
❮ ❯

Step 1: Find the fly

Finding the fly is very important. The easiest way to locate the fly is to listen for their buzzing and try and lore them out on the window.

Step 2: Grab the cup

The cup will be used to trap the fly so you can easily move it outside, use a clear cup to see it inside.

Step 3: Throw it outside

You want to slide a piece of paper under the cup with the fly inside, then you pick it up together and then open your cup outside your home.
